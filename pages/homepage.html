ABOUT
HIJAU SELARAS
Hijau Selaras are an Independent Green Consultant based in Bandung, we are focusing in Green Consultant, Building System Design, Saving Energy Design, Architecture Design, and Interior Design and build.
We have received Green Building Council certification, and all of our teams have also been certified.
We specialize in designing hospital interiors.
Hijau Selaras was founded by Tantri Oktavia in 2006, which started as a project as a green consultant to accompany one of the architects to perfect the existing design to become a green building. After that, he worked on many projects in building design and as a green consultant, and he became one of the nominees in international green competitions. In 2008, Hijau Selaras began designing and contracting both interiors and architecture.
With an interest in green design, I developed myself to study and explore green design at the doctoral level and become an internationally recognized green professional. Currently, apart from being a lecturer in architecture and interiors at Maranatha Christian University, he focuses on design, technology, structures, and green design.
Together with a team of certified experts, we continue to develop to create better designs and build in harmony with the environment.
